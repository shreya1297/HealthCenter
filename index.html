VISION

The mission of the Thapar University Student Health Center is to meet the health care needs of Thapar University students, faculty and staff. Helping students stay healthy is our top priority, knowing that wellness contributes to academic and personal success. The Health Center is committed to promoting the health and wellness of the campus community by providing high-quality health care, prevention and education. Our staff includes board certified doctors, physicians, nurses and assistants. The health center facilitates free checkup and issue of medicines. Students are encouraged to utilize the services of the Health Center and make it an integral part of their university support system. The Health Center is committed to working with students to create a safe and positive environment that will meet their health care needs.
